About Me

My name is Scott Lawless. I try to be well rounded but I am somewhat easily defined as an avid gamer a my core. I play games frequently and it is an activity I enjoy as much as any other that I've been fortunate enough to try. My "main game" is League of Legends and I will regularly play 10 or more games per week. I also play many other games a few shooters, a few rts games as well as a grand strategy game in eu4 (7000 hours played in 8-10 years this game is addicting).

I am also heavily interested in e-sports (with the main title I watch being League of Legends again). I also watch counterstrike and dota2 esports(even though I'm rather bad at each) and as well watch quite a few different fighting game scenes(which I'm even worse at). I really find it awe-inspireing and entertaining. It's oft-maligned in popular media but I think they lack the gamer perspective to be able to appreciate what goes in to really big plays or really clever strategies.

Outside of gaming I am also a huge dog person. I grew up around many dogs (and some cats) because my mom worked as a puppy-raiser and trainer for a service dog company. I currently Have one dog and he is a black-lab named Levi(pictures coming soon).
